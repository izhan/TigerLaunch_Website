It all starts with one great idea.

TigerLaunch is a tiered startup competition that connects the strongest entrepreneurial students from all over the country with investors, mentors, and other resources essential to success. We work with collegiate students with companies as early as a simple vision and guide them to build an minimum viable product and gain real traction and standing.

  • Connect college entrepreneurs from across the country with mentors and resources
  • Help to develop a minimum viable product and pitch deck to prove viability
  • The best of the best fly to our conference to present demos and pitches directly to top investors scoring funding, seed-investments, and powerful connections
